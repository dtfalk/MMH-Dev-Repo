Moms love us. Soon you will too.

My Mom Is Here — formed by Columbia University grads Sam Seiff (vocals), Nick Sare (keys), Aidan Speckhard (drums), Ryan Daar (bass), and Stephen Park (guitar)—made their performance debut on a Morningside Heights rooftop in 2023 and have since evolved into a genre-defying five-piece known for high-energy performances, tight musicianship, and goofy onstage antics.

They’ve packed out iconic NYC venues like Mercury Lounge, Nublu, and Baby’s All Right. In August 2025, the band embarks on their first national tour in support of their debut EP, Baby Steps.

The EP spans from the bittersweet acoustic single “You Just Won’t Give Me Love” to the sci-fi-inspired funk banger “Take Me To Your Leader,” complete with a music video starring Sam’s real-life grandma. From nostalgic mashups to innovative fusion originals, My Mom Is Here delivers a set that’s heartfelt and a whole lot of fun.
